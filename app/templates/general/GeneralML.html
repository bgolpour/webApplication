Artificial Intelligence And Marketing

Nowadays the artificial intelligence solutions together with data science and business analytics solutions such as Business Intelligence systems, Big data and data mining play crucial role in the management of many con- temporary business organizations. The multitude of its benefits include improvement of the whole management process of business organization and especially the process of decision making, allowing for automation of tasks in many areas (D.Jelonek).

According to Arnaud De Bruyn and colleges: Artificial Intelligence will have a deep impact on predictive tasks that can be automated and require little explainability.

The artificial intelligence solutions may embrace the whole set of data science and mining methods, techniques and tools as well as classical and advanced statistical methods such as classification with the application of decision trees and rule- based methods, k-nearest neighbor, linear discrimination, regression analysis connected with data prognosis for specific variables or cluster analysis (T.Munakata).

In Supervised, Unsupervised, and Reinforced sections, I tried to cover a thorough explanation about these Machine Learning paradigms and also some examples of their application in Marketing context.
