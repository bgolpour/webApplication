Introduction

About Me, and This Website

I am a Ph.D. in Business Administration (Marketing). My assertation concerned Service failures Recovery Strategies. In that research, we evaluated more than 4000 service consumers to extract the most effective recovery strategies. 4000 individuals seem satisfactory at the time but who doesn’t agree that a bigger sample could end in a more comprehensive result. Considering the major limitation of some statistical packages in analyzing a very large data set, I decided to join a data analytics program in University of Toronto. I finished it right before the pandemic and during the pandemic and luckdown I had enough time to finished some articles in consumer behavior and market research and more importantly study deeply in data analytic usages in the marketing context. Here, on this website, I’ll try to pass on the knowledge with the easiest way to learn some of the data analytics concepts and explain how they can be used in market research and consumer behavior studies.
